AI in Education

Summary

As a teacher you should know the education field is always an ever-changing field. From the introduction of Calculators to the classroom too personal computers and in the present Generative AI.

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to persuade educators to use Generative AI to enhance their course curriculum and teaching methods.

What is Generative AI?

“Generative AI is a type of artificial intelligence that uses machine learning to generate new content by analyzing and processing vast amounts of data from diverse sources. Generative AI tools can generate text, images, video, sound, code, mathematical calculations, and more.” (Troy Heaps, 2024)

Steps to Get Started

  1. Gather Your Information: Collect all the material you plan to cover during a class, such as course notes, discussions, and activities.
  2. Prepare Your Information: Write your information in a digital tool like Notepad, Word, or an AI chat platform. Include details such as date, time, module, class type (online or in-person), scheduling details, discussions, activities, and readings.
  3. Get Creative and Personalize: Modify your prompts to suit your personal needs. For example, you can designate tasks as mandatory or optional, include pre-class preparations, and add follow-up tasks.
  4. Navigating to Your Chosen AI: Use a generative AI platform like ChatGPT. Log in or create an account, and provide your prepared information as a prompt.
  5. Getting Your Result: Review the AI-generated lesson plan, check for errors, and make any necessary adjustments. Then share it with your students.

Safety Notices

  • Be cautious of unintentional plagiarism or cheating when using Generative AI tools.
  • Overreliance on Generative AI may lead to a loss of critical skills.
  • Generative AI may not always be 100% accurate or unbiased, as the training data might contain inherent biases.
